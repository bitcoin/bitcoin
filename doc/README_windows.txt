Intro
-----
Gameunits is a free open source project derived from Bitcoin, with
the goal of providing a long-term energy-efficient scrypt-based crypto-currency.
Built on the foundation of Bitcoin, PPCoin, NovaCoin, BlackCoin and Shadowcoin innovations such as proof-of-stake
help further advance the field of crypto-currency.
